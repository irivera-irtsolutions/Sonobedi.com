sheet 01 — studio

built around the room,
not the catalog.

every piece starts as a conversation, not a product listing. we measure the actual space, talk through how you'll really use the piece, and design around the real constraints — a stair turn, a low ceiling, a left-handed reader, a dog that needs to fit underneath.

then it's built by hand, one piece at a time: solid wood, real joinery, finishes mixed to match. no veneer over particleboard, no two pieces exactly alike.

sheet 03 — process

from sketch to install.

a real sequence, three stages, usually 6–10 weeks depending on complexity.

step 01

measure & talk

we visit the space — or do it over a call — take real measurements, and talk through how the piece actually needs to function day to day.

step 02

design & sample

sketches and a 3d model come first. then physical material samples — wood species, metal finish, upholstery — land in your hands before anything is cut.

step 03

build & install

built by hand in the workshop, then delivered and installed in the room it was designed for.
